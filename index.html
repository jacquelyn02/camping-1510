Hello and welcome to my Website!

My name is Jacquelyn but most people call me Jackie. The purpose of this website is to inform people of my future events and what activities I have planned for said events.

My planned locations are of course set in Georgia, some camping grounds I would love to cover would be Indian Springs State Park and High Falls State Park. I can't wait to meet you there.

Now for the fun activities I have planned, I have something for both the children and adults to enjoy. Ranging from a 4-hour fishing session where children and their guardians can learn how to fish. We will be covering the basics of course so new learners are free to join.

For the children only I have a grand campfire story to tell them once the sun goes down and when the day breaks I have a nice little scavenger hunt for them to take part in; each day with different items to look for it's sure to be fun! Not to mention a play hunting game where I made little paper mache animals for the children to hunt down if they so wished. The first child to reach 10 points gets a special prize!

Now for the adults I have a more advanced fishing class for anyone interested it is best that you at least have the basics down before coming down for the class. I have a few hiking areas we can venture into also, I hope you bought your best shoes for this trip!
