Greetings again as stated on the main home page this website is about camping and anything else that comes with it such as fishing and hiking!

I made this to reach anyone interested in learning and growing with me as I make this journey into new territory since camping is new to me. I made this to reach new and veteran campers when it comes to taking on the outdoors.

People new to camping, such as myself, can struggle with me as we try to put up a tent, making a flame with flint and steel, and most likely getting lost while venturing in the woods; gotta make sure we have our maps!

Veterns can lead us in the correct direction, everything is a learning experience of course, but with their guided help when needed anything is possible. I can't wait to learn from them and listen to their stories around a campfire!

As I and new people take the tips we learn from these experts we can hopefully teach people who are in the same situation as us. We can give them a push in the right direction and give them useful trips when it comes to camping.

I pride myself on my honesty, resourcefulness, and high value in safety so I can't wait to meet you on our journey.
